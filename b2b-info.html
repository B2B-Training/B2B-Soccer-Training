How B2B Training Works

Everything you need to know about our sessions, what to expect, and how we help players develop their skills at every level.

Private 1-on-1 Sessions

Our bread and butter. One-on-one training is the fastest way to improve because every minute is focused on you. We assess your strengths and weaknesses, then build a custom plan to level up your game.

  • Personalized drills based on your position and goals
  • Immediate feedback and coaching cues
  • Progress tracking session to session
  • Great for tryout prep, season training, or off-season development

Small Group Training

Train with 2-4 players for a competitive, game-like environment. Small groups let us keep the quality high while adding the intensity and fun of working alongside other players.

  • Competitive drills and small-sided games
  • Great for teammates or friends who want to train together
  • Build chemistry and communication on the field

What to Expect

Every session is structured but flexible. We come prepared with a plan, but we also adjust based on what we see and what you need that day. Here's what a typical session looks like:

  • Warm-up with the ball (technical foundation)
  • Focused skill work (based on your development plan)
  • Game-realistic scenarios and pressure drills
  • Cool-down and feedback on what to work on before next session

Who We Train

B2B is open to players of all ages and skill levels. Whether you're just getting started or preparing for high school/college tryouts, we meet you where you are and push you to where you want to be.

  • Youth players (ages 6+) looking to build a foundation
  • Club and travel players preparing for showcases
  • High school players competing for varsity spots
  • Any player looking to get better and have fun doing it
